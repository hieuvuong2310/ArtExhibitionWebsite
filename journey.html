Journey

Introduction

Welcome to my webpage.

This website is for users/viewers to interact with website to decide which scene they want to visit and create your own story/feeling based on the available scenes.

How to use

As a user you can have a choice to choose which scene you want to go next by clicking to the drop-down list and choose the scene you want

However, I decided to keep the scene anonymous. You will not know what the next scene you choose is about until you choose to visit it.

Character

This brown bear will be the main character in all scenes. I chose it because I wanted to keep the consistence of all my videos.

Why brown bear? Because it is related to my childhood, and till now. I am attracted to this cute character. I also want to reflect myself, my interest in my artwork. But this time, I will not make a fixed artwork, I make an interactive art, where viewers can decide what my artwork will be.
